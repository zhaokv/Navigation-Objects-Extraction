MATTHEW WILLIAMSON SHOW

By LISA MARSH

Loading new images...

February 7, 2007 -- Matthew Williamson knows what his women like -- and offered it up over and over again on the runway at Bryant Park.

Brightly colored floaty chiffon dresses were the predominant styles from this young Englishman. The details of Williamson's prints and colorful beadwork, his forte, were really quite remarkable, they were so intricate. Though a flowing caftan is not for all, these dresses and gowns could be.

As a side note, he showed a beaded jacket with beaded knickers (or briefs), not unlike what Sienna Miller wore to her "Factory Girl" premiere party. Maybe she WAS onto something.
