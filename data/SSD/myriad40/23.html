Cheryl and Ashley Cole to 'marry' again

Tuesday, October 28, 2008

The couple plan to renew their wedding vows

They haven't even been married for three years yet, but Ashley and Cheryl Cole are already planning to renew their wedding vows.

The couple's marriage was rocked when it was reported that Ashley, 27, cheated on his 25-year-old wife with a blond hairdresser in January this year.

After a media frenzy the Girls Aloud star and the Chelsea defender patched up the cracks in their relationship to and finally put their marriage back on the tracks.

We're going to build our married life together, not thinking about what if it ever ends

But after their multi-million pound wedding, they are now planning to hold a low-key secret ceremony with family and friends to rekindle their love.

Cheryl told OK! magazine: We're a work in progress.

We're going to build our married life together, not thinking about what if it ever ends.

I wasn't looking for a relationship when I met Ashley and certainly not a long-term one. But pretty soon after I met him I knew something was different and I knew I had never felt that before.

He does little things now that he doesn't even know he's doing. My heart goes and I just think, God you're so perfect.
